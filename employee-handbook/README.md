# 👔 Employee Handbook
 
> *Welcome aboard. Please read this document in full. There will not be a quiz, but there will be a follow-up meeting about the quiz.*
 
## 👋 Welcome to Cubert Coin 
 
If you are reading this, you have probably survived at least one meeting that should have been an email.
 
You are qualified. Your employee badge has been approved.
 
This handbook outlines our culture, values, community structure, and career path within the Corporate Survivors movement. Think of it as your onboarding packet — except this one is actually worth reading.
 
## 📖 The Origin Story
 
Cubert created this coin during a meeting that should have been an email.
 
Nobody noticed.
 
What started as a quiet act of rebellion became a community for millions of professionals navigating spreadsheets, KPIs, deadlines, performance reviews, and meetings about meetings.
 
Today, Cubert Coin represents **Corporate Survivors everywhere**.
 
## 🎯 Our Mission
 
> Build the largest community of Corporate Survivors on the internet.
> **Turn burnout into belonging.**
 
## 💡 What is Proof of Burnout?
 
**Proof of Burnout** is the philosophy behind Cubert Coin.
 
It recognizes the effort, patience, resilience, and humor required to survive modern corporate life.
 
Every spreadsheet. Every KPI. Every deadline. Every meeting that could have been an email.
 
**You earned it.**

## 🏦 Corporate Funds
 
To ensure long-term sustainability, Cubert Coin maintains four dedicated funds. Each serves a specific operational purpose.
 
### 💧 Liquidity Pool
Provides market liquidity and supports healthy trading conditions. Liquidity is locked at launch.
 
### 🏛️ Corporate Treasury
Reserved for future development, operations, and strategic opportunities. Managed through multi-signature controls.
 
### 🌱 Community Growth Fund
Dedicated to community initiatives, events, partnerships, and ecosystem growth. Built to support the long-term expansion of the Corporate Survivors movement.
 
### 👥 Team Allocation
Allocated to contributors helping build and maintain the Cubert Coin ecosystem. Structured to encourage long-term commitment and sustainable development.
 
> For detailed allocation percentages, lock periods, and vesting schedules, refer to the official [Tokenomics](../tokenomics/) documentation.
