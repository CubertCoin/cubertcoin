## 🔍 Transparency Commitments
 
Cubert Coin is committed to operating openly. The following will be disclosed to the community:
 
- Treasury wallet address
- Team wallet address(es)
- Liquidity lock proof and duration
- Any future allocation changes, communicated in advance
All on-chain activity is publicly verifiable on the Solana blockchain.
 
## 🧭 Guiding Principles
 
| Principle | What it means in practice |
|---|---|
| 🔍 Transparency | Wallets, locks, and decisions are disclosed publicly |
| ⏳ Long-Term Alignment | Team tokens vest slowly; no one cashes out on day one |
| 📈 Sustainable Growth | Treasury is managed conservatively for the project's future |
| 🤝 Community First | The largest allocations serve the community, not insiders |
