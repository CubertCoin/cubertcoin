# 👔 Code of Conduct

> *"Conference Room B has rules. So does this repository."*

**Filing Ref.:** COC-001   
**Effective Date:** Upon commit to `main`   
**Version:** 1.0   
**Status:** Active 🟢   

This Code of Conduct applies to all CubertCoin community spaces — including this GitHub repository, Discord, Telegram, Reddit, X, and any other official channel. By participating, you agree to abide by it.

## 🤝 Our Commitment

The Corporate Survivors community is built on a simple premise: shared experience creates belonging.

We are committed to making participation in CubertCoin a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We do not tolerate behavior that makes people feel unsafe, unwelcome, or disrespected. This is not a corporate HR policy. It is a community standard we actually mean.

## ✅ Expected Behavior

Corporate Survivors are expected to:

- **Be respectful** — treat others the way you would want to be treated in a meeting you actually chose to attend
- **Be constructive** — criticism is welcome; cruelty is not
- **Be honest** — do not misrepresent yourself, the project, or other community members
- **Be collaborative** — share knowledge, help newcomers, and support the community you want to be part of
- **Be patient** — not everyone has the same level of experience, and that is fine
- **Follow official channels** — do not spread unverified information about $CUBE, the contract address, or the project status

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated in any CubertCoin community space:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination of any kind
- Personal attacks, insults, or derogatory comments
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith participation
- Sharing false or misleading information about the project, the token, or the team
- Impersonating the Founder, Core Contributors, moderators, or official accounts
- Promoting scam tokens, fake contract addresses, or unauthorized projects using CubertCoin IP
- Unsolicited promotion of unrelated projects, services, or products
- Publishing private information about others without explicit permission
- Any other behavior that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate in a professional community

## 🛡️ Enforcement

### Who Enforces This

Core Contributors and designated moderators are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct across all community spaces. The Founder holds final authority on all enforcement decisions.

### What Happens When Rules Are Broken

Violations are handled progressively, based on severity and history:

| Level | Behavior | Consequence |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **1 — Warning** | Minor violation, first offense | Private warning with explanation |
| **2 — Temporary Restriction** | Repeated or moderate violation | Temporary removal from one or more spaces |
| **3 — Permanent Ban** | Severe violation or repeated offenses | Permanent removal from all CubertCoin community spaces |

Scam promotion, impersonation, and fraud bypass the progressive system and result in **immediate permanent ban** across all channels.

### How to Report a Violation

If you witness or experience a violation of this Code of Conduct:

1. **Do not engage** with the offending behavior publicly — escalation rarely helps
2. **Report it** via the official Discord moderation channel, GitHub Issues, or direct message to a Core Contributor or moderator
3. **Include** a description of the incident, the platform, and any relevant screenshots or links

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion. Retaliation against anyone who reports in good faith is itself a violation of this Code of Conduct.

## ⚠️ Scam and Impersonation Policy

CubertCoin operates a zero-tolerance policy on scams and impersonation — particularly fake contract addresses, fake team accounts, and fake giveaways.

> 🔑 No one from CubertCoin will ever DM you first, ask for your seed phrase, private key, or wallet approval. Any message claiming otherwise is a scam.

The official $CUBE contract address will only ever be published via:

## 🌐 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- This GitHub repository (issues, discussions, pull requests, comments)
- The official Discord server
- The official Telegram channel
- The official Reddit community
- The official X / Twitter account mentions and replies
- Any other space where an individual is representing or participating in the CubertCoin community

It also applies to behavior outside official spaces when that behavior has a direct, negative impact on the community or its members.
