# Brand Book

## About the Brand

Cubert Coin is a community-driven project built around corporate culture, workplace humor, and the shared experience of surviving modern office life.

Created during a meeting that should have been an email, Cubert Coin exists to unite Corporate Survivors around the world under a single banner:

Proof of Burnout.

## Brand Identity

**Character**: Cubert.

The official mascot of Cubert Coin.
A corporate survivor navigating meetings, spreadsheets, KPIs, deadlines, performance reviews, and the endless pursuit of productivity.


**Tagline**: Proof of Burnout.

**Mission**: Build the largest community of Corporate Survivors on the internet.

Turn burnout into belonging.

**Brand Personality**

Cubert Coin should always feel:
-	Corporate
-	Satirical
-	Relatable
-	Professional
-	Community-Driven

The tone should be humorous without becoming childish.

**Proof of Burnout.**
