# 📘 Brand Book
 
This document defines the visual identity, voice, and messaging standards for **Cubert Coin**. All community members, contributors, and partners are expected to follow these guidelines when representing the brand.
 
## 🏢 About the Brand
 
Cubert Coin is a community-driven project built around corporate culture, workplace humor, and the shared experience of surviving modern office life.
 
Created during a meeting that should have been an email, Cubert Coin exists to unite Corporate Survivors around the world under a single banner:
 
> *Proof of Burnout.*
 
## 👤 Brand Identity 
 
### Character
 
**Cubert** is the official mascot of Cubert Coin — a Corporate Survivor navigating meetings, spreadsheets, KPIs, deadlines, performance reviews, and the endless pursuit of productivity. He is all of us.
 
### Tagline
 
> **Proof of Burnout.**
 
### Mission
 
> Build the largest community of Corporate Survivors on the internet.
> Turn burnout into belonging.
 
### Brand Personality
 
Cubert Coin should always feel:
 
| Trait | Description |
|---|---|
| 🏢 Corporate | Speaks the language of the office — ironically |
| 😏 Satirical | Pokes fun at corporate culture without punching down |
| 🤝 Relatable | Every interaction should feel like talking to a coworker who gets it |
| 👔 Professional | Polished enough to belong in a boardroom, subversive enough to escape it |
| 🌐 Community-Driven | Built by survivors, for survivors |
 
> The tone should be humorous without becoming childish. Think dry wit, not chaos.

## 🖼️ Logo System
 
### Primary Logo — Hexagon + Cubert + Wordmark
 
The full brand mark. Use this whenever space allows.
 
**Approved Usage**
- Website
- README principal
- Brand Book
- Employee Handbook
- Tokenomics
- Media Kit
- Press releases
 
### Secondary Logo — Coin + Cubert Coin (horizontal layout)

A compact variant optimized for horizontal formats.

**Approved Usage** 
- Banners
- Partner materials
- Headers
- Email signatures
- Promotional materials

### Wordmark — typography only, no icon

Text-only version of the brand mark.

**Approved Usage** 
- Footers
- Website sections
- Graphic materials
- Layouts where the symbol already appears
 
### Icon — Coin only - no text

Designed for maximum legibility at small sizes.

**Approved Usage**
- Favicon
- CoinGecko
- CoinMarketCap
- DexScreener
- Wallets
- Exchanges

### Mascot — Cubert's face - no hexagon, no text
 
The mascot alone, without wordmark.

**Approved Usage** 
- X / Twitter
- Reddit
- Discord
- Telegram
- Instagram
- Memes
- Social content
 
### ❌ Logo Misuse
 
To protect brand consistency, **never**:
 
- Stretch or distort the logo
- Rotate or reposition elements
- Change any official colors
- Add shadows, gradients, or effects
- Modify or redraw the Cubert character
- Place the logo on backgrounds that reduce legibility
 
## 🗣️ Voice & Messaging
 
### Preferred Phrases
 
These phrases define the Cubert Coin vocabulary. Use them consistently across all materials.
 
- *Proof of Burnout.*
- *Another Meeting. Another Token.*
- *Corporate Survivors.*
- *Career Progression.*
- *Corporate Intranet.*
- *Corporate HQ.*
### Always Avoid
 
Cubert Coin is a community project. We do not make financial claims — ever.
 
- ❌ Price promises
- ❌ Financial guarantees
- ❌ Investment advice
- ❌ Unrealistic claims about returns or value
